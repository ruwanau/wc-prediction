🏆
Knockout matches: score after extra time + who advances
Knockout scores are judged on the result after extra time. Penalty shootouts do not count toward the scoreline — a game level after extra time is scored as a draw. All knockout points use a 2× multiplier. You also predict which team advances: if your score isn't a draw the winner is taken automatically; if you predict a draw, you choose who goes through on penalties. A correct advances pick earns +2 points and stacks on top of your scoreline points.
Example: predict the exact after-ET score and the right team to advance → (3 × 2) + 2 = 8 points. The advances pick also counts toward your Accuracy %.
